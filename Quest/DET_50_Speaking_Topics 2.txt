===============================================================
  Duolingo English Test - 50가지 스피킹 주제 (Speaking Sample Topics)
===============================================================

#1  Talk about a hobby or activity that you enjoy in your free time. How did you get started, and why do you like it?
    (여가 시간에 즐기는 취미 활동에 관해 얘기해보세요. 어떻게 시작했으며 좋아하는 이유는?)

#2  Discuss the role of education in society. What do you think should be the primary focus of education systems?
    (사회에서 교육의 역할에 대해 이야기해보세요. 교육 시스템의 주요 초점은 무엇이라고 생각하나요?)

#3  Talk about a person who inspires you. What qualities do they possess that make them inspirational?
    (영감을 주는 사람에 대해 이야기해보세요. 어떤 특징이 영감을 주나요?)

#4  Describe an environmental issue that concerns you. What steps can individuals and governments take to address this issue?
    (우려되는 환경 문제에 대해 설명하세요. 개인과 정부는 어떤 노력을 할 수 있을까요?)

#5  Discuss a cultural custom or tradition from your country. What is its significance and how is it celebrated?
    (당신 나라의 문화적 관습 또는 전통에 관해 이야기해보세요. 의미와 기념 방식은?)

#6  What is your favorite holiday and why? Describe how your family celebrates it and any special traditions related to it.
    (가장 좋아하는 공휴일과 이유는? 가족의 기념 방식과 특별한 전통은?)

#7  Explain the importance of friendship in your life. How do your friends help you grow and support you in difficult times?
    (인생에서 우정의 중요성을 설명하세요. 친구들은 어떻게 성장을 돕고 힘든 시기에 지원하나요?)

#8  What are the advantages and disadvantages of using public transportation versus driving a personal car for everyday commuting?
    (출퇴근에 대중교통과 자차의 장단점은?)

#9  Describe your favorite type of weather and explain how it affects your mood and daily activities.
    (좋아하는 날씨와 그것이 기분·일상에 미치는 영향을 설명하세요.)

#10 If you could travel to any country in the world, which one would you choose and why? What attracts you to that country?
    (어떤 나라든 갈 수 있다면 어디를 선택하며 이유는? 그 나라의 매력은?)

#11 What is your favorite piece of technology and why? How has it improved your life and the lives of others?
    (가장 좋아하는 기술과 이유는? 그 기술이 삶을 어떻게 개선했나요?)

#12 Talk about a historical figure you admire. What do you find inspiring about their accomplishments and life story?
    (존경하는 역사적 인물에 대해 이야기해보세요. 업적과 인생사의 어떤 점이 영감을 주나요?)

#13 Discuss a book, movie, or show that made a significant impact on you. What was the story about, and what emotions did it evoke?
    (큰 영향을 끼친 책/영화/방송에 대해 이야기해보세요. 내용과 어떤 감정을 일으켰나요?)

#14 Talk about a skill that you have learned or would like to learn in the future. How will it benefit you in your personal or professional life?
    (배운 기술 또는 미래에 배우고 싶은 기술은? 개인적·직업적으로 어떻게 도움이 될까요?)

#15 Describe a time when you learned an important lesson from a family member or close friend. What was the lesson, and how has it influenced your decisions?
    (가족이나 친한 친구에게서 중요한 교훈을 배운 경험은? 그 교훈이 결정에 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#16 Discuss a subject you're passionate about and explain why you find it interesting and how you continue learning about it.
    (열정적인 과목을 말하고, 흥미를 느끼는 이유와 어떻게 계속 배우고 있는지 설명하세요.)

#17 Describe a time when you learned an important lesson. What happened, and how has this lesson influenced your decisions and actions?
    (중요한 교훈을 배운 경험을 설명하세요. 무슨 일이 있었고, 그 교훈이 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#18 Describe a favorite dish from your culture. What are its ingredients, and how is it prepared? What makes it special to you?
    (문화의 좋아하는 요리를 설명하세요. 재료와 조리법은? 특별한 이유는?)

#19 Discuss a movie or movie series that made a significant impact on you. What was the story about, and what emotions did it evoke?
    (큰 영향을 끼친 영화/영화 시리즈에 대해 이야기해보세요. 내용과 감정은?)

#20 Describe a time when you had to make a difficult decision. What factors did you consider, and how did you reach your decision?
    (어려운 결정을 내린 경험은? 어떤 요인을 고려했고, 어떻게 결정했나요?)

#21 Explain the importance of learning a new language. How can it benefit individuals and society?
    (새로운 언어를 배우는 것의 중요성을 설명하세요. 개인과 사회에 어떻게 도움이 될까요?)

#22 Talk about a skill that you possess. How did you develop this skill, and how do you use it in your everyday life?
    (가지고 있는 기술에 대해 이야기하세요. 어떻게 발전시켰고, 일상에서 어떻게 사용하나요?)

#23 Describe a place you have visited that had a significant impact on you. What made the experience memorable?
    (큰 영향을 미친 방문 장소는? 그 경험이 기억에 남는 이유는?)

#24 Discuss the role of technology in modern society. How has it improved our lives, and what challenges does it present?
    (현대 사회에서 기술의 역할에 대해 이야기하세요. 삶을 어떻게 개선했고 어떤 문제가 있나요?)

#25 Discuss the importance of environmental conservation. How can we protect our planet for future generations?
    (환경 보전의 중요성에 대해 이야기하세요. 미래 세대를 위해 어떻게 지구를 보호할 수 있을까요?)

#26 Talk about a skill that you would like to learn in the future. How will it benefit you in your personal or professional life?
    (미래에 배우고 싶은 기술은? 개인적·직업적으로 어떻게 도움이 될까요?)

#27 If you could spend a day with any historical figure, who would it be and why? What questions would you ask them, and what would you like to learn from them?
    (역사적 인물과 하루를 보낼 수 있다면 누구를 선택하며 이유는? 어떤 질문을 하고 무엇을 배우고 싶나요?)

#28 Talk about an accomplishment or goal you have achieved. What challenges did you face, and how did you overcome them to reach your goal?
    (성취한 목표에 대해 이야기하세요. 어떤 어려움이 있었고 어떻게 극복했나요?)

#29 Describe a time when you had to adapt to a new environment or situation. What challenges did you face, and how did you overcome them?
    (새로운 환경에 적응해야 했던 경험은? 어떤 어려움이 있었고 어떻게 극복했나요?)

#30 Describe a favorite childhood memory and explain why it is so special to you.
    (좋아하는 어린 시절의 기억과 그것이 특별한 이유를 설명하세요.)

#31 Describe an animal that you find fascinating. What do you find interesting about it?
    (매력적인 동물에 대해 설명하세요. 왜 흥미롭다고 생각하나요?)

#32 What are the advantages and disadvantages of using public transportation versus driving a personal car for everyday commuting?
    (일상 출퇴근에서 대중교통과 자차의 장단점은?)

#33 Describe a typical day in your life. What activities and tasks do you usually do during the day?
    (일상적인 하루를 설명하세요. 보통 어떤 활동과 일을 하나요?)

#34 Describe a historical event that you find interesting. Why does it interest you, and what impact did it have on the world?
    (흥미로운 역사적 사건을 설명하세요. 왜 흥미롭고 세계에 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#35 Describe a memorable event from your childhood. What happened, and why do you remember it so vividly?
    (어린 시절 기억에 남는 사건을 설명하세요. 무슨 일이 있었고 왜 생생히 기억하나요?)

#36 Talk about your favorite kind of music and explain why you enjoy it. How does it make you feel when you listen to it?
    (좋아하는 음악 종류와 이유를 설명하세요. 들을 때 어떤 기분이 드나요?)

#37 Describe a piece of technology that has made a significant impact on your life. How has it changed the way you do things?
    (삶에 큰 영향을 미친 기술을 설명하세요. 일상을 어떻게 바꿨나요?)

#38 Discuss a cultural custom or tradition from your country. What is its significance and how is it celebrated?
    (나라의 문화적 관습이나 전통에 대해 이야기하세요. 의미와 기념 방식은?)

#39 Describe a memorable meal you have had. What made it special, and what did you eat?
    (기억에 남는 식사를 설명하세요. 무엇이 특별했고 무엇을 먹었나요?)

#40 Discuss the importance of communication in today's world. How has technology affected the way we communicate?
    (오늘날 통신의 중요성에 대해 이야기하세요. 기술이 소통 방식에 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#41 Describe your favorite food and explain what makes it special. How does it connect to your culture or personal experiences?
    (좋아하는 음식과 특별한 이유를 설명하세요. 문화나 개인 경험과 어떻게 연결되나요?)

#42 Talk about a hobby or activity that you enjoy in your free time. How did you get started, and why do you like it?
    (여가 시간에 즐기는 취미에 대해 이야기하세요. 어떻게 시작했고 왜 좋아하나요?)

#43 Describe a memorable trip or travel experience. What did you learn, and what made it special?
    (기억에 남는 여행 경험을 설명하세요. 무엇을 배웠고 무엇이 특별했나요?)

#44 Talk about a historical figure you admire. What do you find inspiring about their accomplishments and life story?
    (존경하는 역사적 인물에 대해 이야기하세요. 업적과 인생사의 어떤 점이 영감을 주나요?)

#45 Describe a memorable experience you had while traveling. What made it stand out, and how did it impact you?
    (여행 중 기억에 남는 경험을 설명하세요. 무엇이 특별했고 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#46 Describe a hobby or interest of yours that has evolved over time. How did it start, and how has it changed?
    (시간이 지나며 변화한 취미나 관심사를 설명하세요. 어떻게 시작했고 어떻게 변했나요?)

#47 Describe a time when you had to work as part of a team. What was your role, and what was the outcome of the teamwork?
    (팀으로 일한 경험을 설명하세요. 역할은 무엇이었고 결과는 어땠나요?)

#48 Describe a positive experience from your school days that had a lasting impact on you.
    (학창 시절 긍정적인 경험 중 오래도록 영향을 미친 것을 설명하세요.)

#49 Discuss a book, movie, or TV show that has inspired or influenced you. What was it about, and how did it affect your perspective?
    (영감을 준 책/영화/TV 방송에 대해 이야기하세요. 내용과 관점에 어떤 영향을 미쳤나요?)

#50 Talk about a person who has had a significant influence on your life. How have they impacted you, and what have you learned from them?
    (삶에 큰 영향을 미친 사람에 대해 이야기하세요. 어떤 영향을 미쳤고 무엇을 배웠나요?)
